About Southern California Well Service

Family-owned. Locally operated. Here when you need us.

Our Story

Southern California Well Service started with a simple idea: provide honest, reliable well service to our neighbors. After working in the industry and seeing how some companies operated — hidden fees, unnecessary repairs, slow response times — we knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we're proud to be the well service company that San Diego, Riverside, and San Bernardino County residents trust. When you call us, you're not getting a call center — you're getting real people who live and work in your community.

We answer our phones. We show up when we say we will. We explain what's wrong in plain English and give you options — not just the most expensive one. And we stand behind our work.

What We Stand For

Honesty First

We'll tell you exactly what's wrong and what it'll cost to fix — before we start any work. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Respect Your Time

When we say we'll be there, we'll be there. Your time matters, and we won't waste it with vague appointment windows.

Quality Work

We use quality parts and do the job right the first time. A quick fix that fails in a month isn't a fix — it's a waste of your money.

Community Focus

We live here. We raise our families here. When we help our neighbors, we're building the community we want to be part of.
