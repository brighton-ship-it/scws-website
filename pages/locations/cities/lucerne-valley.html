Why Choose Us for Lucerne Valley Well Service?

Lucerne Valley sits at 2,900 feet elevation in a large desert basin between the San Bernardino Mountains and the Mojave Desert. The valley's deep alluvial deposits require drilling to significant depths to reach the regional aquifer. Water is precious here, and wells are the only source.

We're Lucerne Valley well specialists. From Rabbit Dry Lake to the properties along Highway 247, from the rural homesteads near Old Woman Springs Road to the ranches in the valley floor, we've worked throughout Lucerne Valley. We understand desert aquifer systems, know the depth to water across the valley, and have the equipment for deep desert drilling.

When you live in Lucerne Valley, your well is your lifeline in a remote desert environment. Choose a company that knows deep desert wells and has proven success in challenging conditions.
