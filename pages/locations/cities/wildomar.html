Wildomar's Rural Character

Wildomar retained its rural character even after incorporating as a city in 2008. The area is popular with horse owners and those seeking larger lots and a country lifestyle. Many of these properties rely on private wells rather than municipal water connections.

The Murrieta Creek runs through the area, and the alluvial deposits along its path create good aquifer conditions. We've been drilling and servicing wells in what's now Wildomar for years, understanding the local geology and water conditions.
