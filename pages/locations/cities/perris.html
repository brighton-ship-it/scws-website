Perris Valley Agriculture

Perris has long been an agricultural center of Riverside County. While development has transformed much of the area, dairy farms, horse properties, and crop land still define large portions of the valley. All of this agriculture depends on groundwater.

The Perris Valley aquifer provides good water at reasonable depths. Most wells range from 200-500 feet. We've served Perris Valley farmers and ranchers for years, understanding the demands that agricultural operations place on well systems.
