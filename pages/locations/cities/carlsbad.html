Carlsbad's Agricultural Heritage

Carlsbad's flower fields are world-famous, and many of these agricultural operations still rely on private wells. The ranunculus fields, nurseries, and remaining strawberry farms need consistent, high-volume water that only wells can provide reliably.

The geology here includes coastal sediments and the Santiago Formation. Saltwater intrusion is a consideration for wells near the coast, but proper well design and depth selection prevents these issues. We've drilled and serviced wells throughout Carlsbad and understand the local aquifer dynamics.
