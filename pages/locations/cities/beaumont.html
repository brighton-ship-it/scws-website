Beaumont's Growth & Water

Beaumont has grown dramatically in recent years, but the area's agricultural heritage remains. Cherry Valley's orchards and the rural properties around town still depend on private wells, even as new developments connect to municipal water.

The Beaumont Basin aquifer provides excellent groundwater, recharged by mountain runoff from the San Bernardino National Forest. We've been drilling and servicing wells in Beaumont and Cherry Valley for years, watching the community grow while maintaining the water systems that sustain it.
