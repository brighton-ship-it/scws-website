Riverside County Well Conditions

Understanding local geology helps us serve you better:

Temecula Valley

Decomposed granite with alluvial valleys. Many wells serve vineyards and horse properties. Depths typically 200-500 feet.

San Jacinto Valley

Mix of alluvial deposits and fractured rock. Agricultural irrigation demands require careful pump sizing.

Mountain Communities

Anza, Aguanga, and Idyllwild have hard rock formations requiring specialized drilling. Wells can be 400-800+ feet.

Water Quality

Some areas have elevated mineral content. We recommend testing and can advise on treatment options.
