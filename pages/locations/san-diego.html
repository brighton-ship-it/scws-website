San Diego County Well Conditions

San Diego County has diverse geology that affects well drilling and performance:

Granite Formations

Much of the backcountry sits on decomposed granite. Wells often tap into fractures in the bedrock. Depths typically range from 300-600 feet.

Valley Alluvium

Valley areas like Ramona and Valley Center have alluvial deposits that can provide excellent water yields at shallower depths.

Coastal Influence

Properties closer to the coast may encounter different water chemistry. Proper testing is essential.

Drought Considerations

Water levels fluctuate with drought cycles. We monitor conditions and can advise on pump settings and water conservation.
