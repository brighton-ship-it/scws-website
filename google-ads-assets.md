## Upload Instructions

1. Go to Google Ads → Campaigns → Your Performance Max Campaign
2. Click "Asset groups" in the left menu
3. Click on your asset group (or create new)
4. Add the headlines, descriptions above
5. Upload images (need to create/gather these)
6. Add logo
7. Save and let Google optimize

The more assets you provide, the better Google can optimize delivery.
