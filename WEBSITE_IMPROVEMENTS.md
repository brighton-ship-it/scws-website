### Competitor/Inspiration Analysis

**Key Modern Contractor Website Elements (from research):**
1. **Mobile-First Design** - Site must work flawlessly on phones
2. **Bold Branding** - Move beyond traditional blue-only palettes
3. **Instant Booking/CTA** - Prominent "Call Now" or "Emergency Service" buttons
4. **Trust Signals** - Reviews, licenses, team photos prominently displayed
5. **Clear Services List** - Easy navigation to specific services

**Top Design Principles:**
- Minimalism with effective use of white space
- High-quality, REAL imagery (not stock photos)
- Fast-loading pages
- Family-owned/local emphasis works well

**Inspiration Sites:**
- Solv Plumbing (Australia) - Bold navy/white, modern feel
- Aspen Mountain Plumbing - Family-owned focus, image upload feature
- Mr. Rooter - Conversion-focused, clear emergency CTAs
- Dribbble "plumbing-website" tag - 150+ modern design concepts
